Company Profile

Material & Craft

Engineered from
the inside out.

Every alloy, every surface treatment, every internal component is selected for longevity over shortcut. Our Ceramic Disc cartridges are rated to 500,000 operating cycles.

01
Solid Brass Body
C36000 free-machining brass. Superior corrosion resistance and dimensional stability across temperature extremes.
02
PVD Surface Technology
Physical Vapour Deposition coatings — 5× harder than standard electroplating. Scratch and tarnish resistant for life.
03
Ceramic Disc Cartridge
Precision-lapped ceramic discs provide smooth, drip-free operation across 500,000+ operating cycles.
04
316 Marine Grade Steel
SPA series exclusive. Molybdenum-enhanced — preferred for coastal and high-humidity environments.
